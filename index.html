"Why "Café Independent", you may ask? Because we ourselves prepare all our baked goods from kneading dough to decorating with berries, we ourselves grow the products from which we make our breakfasts and ourselves import coffee from the most distant corners of the planet. And all this is just to make sure that our visitors can be free to start their day with real art, and not dull reality."

About our place

Café Independent is a cozy home bakery, because everything is simple and delicious here, just like at home. We do not use recipes that are complex in composition (there are no more than 10 ingredients in any dessert), we do not add preservatives. We produce all the necessary components ourselves, we even cook caramel for cakes ourselves! Many things are intentionally made entirely by hand, for example, honey cake dough, which we know tastes better!

The absence of preservatives significantly reduces the shelf life of our confectionery masterpieces, so in our cafes we always offer pies and cakes prepared this morning. In cooking, we also do not change our main principle: we use only natural ingredients that are grown on our own plantations.
